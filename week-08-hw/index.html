WAFFLES INVADING OUR WORLD

The Waffles have landed!
Watch out world, extraterrestrial waffles have landed on our planet from a faraway galaxy, and they aren't just the kind of waffle that we're used to. These waffles are alive, and power hungry.
Oh yeah, and they taste terrible too!
Not only that, these waffles are military trained, and have fought off the Air Force, Army, Navy, and soon to be Marines!
All people are advised to stay indoors, and if you see any waffles nearby call this number: 1-800-PANCAKES.
